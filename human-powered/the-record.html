The Record

On September 22, 1992 in Colorado's San Luis Valley, the Cheetah sprinted to an average speed of 68.73 MPH (110.68 kilometers per hour) setting a new world speed record for HPVs.

This event allows a virtually unlimited run up to a 200 meter speed trap. Chris (The Punisher) Huber rode the Cheetah to an average speed of 68.73 mph through the speed trap. The record was set at about 6:55 PM mountain standard time on September 22, 1992. The course was on Six Mile Lane which is a flat road which crosses a Bison preserve in the San Luis valley, Colorado. Of course, the Cheetah is named after the fast cat of the same name.
